Jokes, the Great Communicator

Laughter is innate to all of us, which is why Dad Jokes LLC sought out a way to bring families closer together by targeting the corniest jokster of them all, the dad. Allowing the dad to increase his Joke Portfolio in a new revolutionary way.

Say no more, take me there!

Why the Internet?

Throughout history, jokes have been predominately shared orally and via text. We decided, it's time to move the Dad Joke genre to the world wide web to simply allow more dads than ever before to have access to the jokes that will spark laughs (and embarrasment) for their children and spouses.

What Does It Do?

Dad Jokes allows users to create accounts and find new jokes to increase their Joke Portfolio. Using our site, users will find many many jokes they've never heard of, and some that may have slipped their mind, either way Dad Jokes is a great way to bring the funnies in to your family. Whip them out a dinner time, during a picnic, or during baseball practice. Anytime is a good time for a Dad Joke.
